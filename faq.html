Frequently Asked Questions

This is a feedback collection application that allows users to submit their feedback which is then stored and can be viewed in the submissions page.

Submissions are stored in the browser's local storage, which means they persist even after the browser is closed but are specific to the device and browser used.

Currently, there's no edit functionality. You would need to submit a new feedback form if you want to update your information.

Your data is stored locally in your browser and isn't sent to any server. However, anyone with access to your device can view the submissions.

You can clear all submissions by clearing your browser's local storage for this website.
